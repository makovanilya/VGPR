## Timeline
- **2010-07-27**: Need for Speed: World released as a free-to-play MMO by EA.
- **2015-04-15**: EA announces closure, SpeedBoost purchases and new account creation disabled immediately.
- **2015-07-14**: Servers shut down permanently, game becomes completely unlaunchable.
- **2015**: Community offline patch created independently without any publisher involvement.
- **2017**: Soapbox Race World fan-server project launches, restoring multiplayer which was **entirely community-driven**.
- **2020-01-01**: WorldUnited community server launches as the primary SBRW host. 
- **2026-05-03**: Community servers are still active but official product remains permanently dead.
