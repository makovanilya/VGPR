## Timeline
- **2009-06-25**: Battlefield Heroes launched as a free-to-play online multiplayer TPS.
- **2012-01-12**: Game reaches 10 million registered users.
- **2015-04-15**: Easy Studios announces shutdown, new account registration and premium currency purchases disabled immediately. Players with remaining paid currency encouraged to spend it with no refunds offered.
- **2015-07-14**: Servers shut down permanently and game becomes completely unlaunchable.
- **Post-2015**: Community Rising Hub project begins reverse-engineering server infrastructure independently.
- **2025**: An unofficial spiritual successor titled Heroes of Valor begins development.
- **2026-05-03**: Official product permanently dead, EA has released no server files or source code.
