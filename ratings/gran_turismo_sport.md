## Timeline
- **2017-10-17**: Released as PS4 exclusive, launches with Sport Mode but no campaign. GT League campaign added in post-launch update.
- **2023-09-29**: Polyphony Digital announces end of online services effective January 31, 2024; DLC distribution to end December 1, 2023.
- **2023-12-01**: All DLC delisted from PlayStation Store, previously purchased DLC remains accessible.
- **2024-01-??**: Game itself delisted from PlayStation Store without warning.
- **2024-01-31**: Servers shut down and Patch 1.69 is simultaneously released, enabling offline saving and removing the online connection requirement for singleplayer.
- **2026-05-03**: Offline campaign playable for existing owners; Sport Mode permanently gone and game is unobtainable for new players.
